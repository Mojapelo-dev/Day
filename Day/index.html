Melsoft Academy provides students with the following:

  • Career transition into tech for all, aimed at reducing youth unemployment.
  • Three globally recognized certifications for graduates.
  • Comprehensive career support, including interview prep and portfolio development and access to a tech community for networking, mentorship, and exclusive events.

Melsoft Academy is focused on reducing youth unemployment by facilitating a career switch into technology. We offer an engaging learning environment, career support, and opportunities for networking. Our programs ensure graduates receive three globally recognized certifications, fostering an international level of expertise. Students benefit from personal mentorship, guaranteed internships, flexible payment plans, and a 100% online learning platform. This academy aims to build a community among software engineers, coding mentors, and tech entrepreneurs, offering access to exclusive events and job opportunities​.

Markup Languages

Markup languages are systems for annotating a document in a way that is syntactically distinguishable from the text. They tell the software how to display the text or handle data. HTML (Hypertext Markup Language): The standard language for creating and designing web pages and web applications. It structures content on the web.

CSS (Cascading Style Sheets): Used to style and layout web pages, for example, to alter font sizes, colors, spacing, and the layout of elements.

JavaScript: Primarily used for creating dynamic and interactive web content. It runs on the client side of the web browser but can also be used on the server side with environments such as Node.js.

Pricing

Pricing

Melsoft Academy offers two main pricing options for their courses, aiming to cater to different needs and schedules of their students:

Part-Time

This option allows students to study at their own pace, recommended for those who can commit 10-20 hours a week. It's designed to be completed within 6 to 12 months. The pricing for the part-time course is R 29,999 if paid once-off, or R 5,999 per month over six months. Features of this plan include starting anytime, focusing on individual projects, weekly campus visits, pre-recorded lectures, dedicated learning assistants, study finance availability, and a guaranteed internship

Buy Now

Full-Time

The full-time course is an accelerated learning option that runs for 6 months with an intensive schedule requiring 40-60 hours a week. It's cohort-based, encouraging both individual and group projects. Pricing for the full-time course is R 39,999 once-off, or R 7,999 per month over six months. Additional benefits include weekly campus visits, live and pre-recorded lectures, dedicated learning assistants, study finance availability, and a guaranteed internship.
